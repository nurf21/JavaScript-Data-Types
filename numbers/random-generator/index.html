About Uniform Probability

The randomInteger(min, max) function uses the formula: Math.floor(Math.random() * (max - min + 1)) + min. This ensures that every integer between min and max has an equal chance of being selected. For example, between 1 and 5, each number (1, 2, 3, 4, 5) has exactly a 20% probability of being chosen.
